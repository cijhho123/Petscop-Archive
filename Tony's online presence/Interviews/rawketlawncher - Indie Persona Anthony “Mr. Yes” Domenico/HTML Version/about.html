About Rawket Lawncher

Rawket Lawncher is basically a simple blog about games&technology. Not only just news or reviews, but also about individualism. The blog is maintained -sometimes miserably- by yours truly ,Square Tetromino. I actually have my main blog here. It’s a personal collection of freeware&open-source games for PC and Linux. Based on this “individualistic” approach, I decided to cover other gaming related content here; which I wouldn’t be able to post there because it simply wouldn’t fit in.

Articles and other content that you see here are my personal collection of gaming/digital culture. In this state of information exchange availability through internet, I think it is better for everyone to share what is important/interesting to them and to let vast amounts of information circulate around masses. By this way, we won’t rely on news sources that are small in numbers; increase the alternatives of where to get information and be able to rearrange those information based on our preferences and re-publish them accordingly.

This makes Rawket Lawncher an individual, together with tons of other individuals who publish personalised information on various other topics. The idea behind the blog was to reflect this individualism through looks and content. I am not liking where the video gaming/digital culture is going towards so I made my own thing. I prefer uncut, raw material; hence the name “Rawket Lawncher”.

Welcome to Rawket Lawncher, this is counter-culture, a digital molotov.
