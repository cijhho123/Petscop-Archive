PlayNotes: Null Divide+

Alternate Title: Enter the Dragon, with a Spaceship

It’s gameplay time on Rawket Lawncher! I have been fiddling with the html5 version of Null Divide+(ND) for quite some time now and although this is a fairly delayed self-assignment for me, Rawket Lawncher hates deadlines so here we go. The thing that got me interested first with ND was it actually looked pretty close to an original NES/Famicom -ignoring the resolution- game and it resembled The Guardian Legend in terms of mixing space shooter with action-adventure. ND might be lacking that “dual gameplay” mechanic as you don’t switch between vertical shoot’em up and dungeon exploration modes but still it has some goodies to keep you playing and has smart level design with properly adjusted difficulty.

PlayNotes: Super Trench Attack

Digital video games have evolved indeed, with everyone struggling to bring the experience closer to the reality, which does not always equal to being better. Luckily, we have this other bunch who want to put entertainment back into the gaming. Retro Army delivers a big box of uncontrolled giggles with their Super Trench Attack (STA); a World War I tale of courage and bravery, with hostile little scimitar wielding monkeys wearing sailor hats.
