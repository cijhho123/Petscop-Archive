FireStarter: Cymbil Spellcraft

Our latest post to quench your pyromaniac tendencies involves a game. Well, it should have; otherwise why would I bother? What is interesting about this project is that it also comes with tools to tailor your own adventure -or to cater to your weird fetishes (NO!)-. Cymbil Spellcraft is a nice looking action RPG with easy customization, waiting for your support.

Although my dirty mind claims otherwise, Cymbil Spellcraft might be the test bed for many well done G rated projects. Its editor does not require programing so enthusiasm is the only thing you need to make your own game. For the more capable crowd; the game will provide advanced modding options, enabling modders to customize nearly every aspect of the game.

If we return to the game itself; it’s a 3D A-RPG but as you might have guessed, you get an isometric/top down view. It has all the goodies of a good -goodies of good, yeah I am well aware of that miserable wordplay- RPG: multiplayer co-op, no-class specific equipment, freedom to use any object as a weapon, boss fights, platforming, puzzles and action -an A-RPG without action, what was I thinking?-.
