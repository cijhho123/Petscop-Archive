#DevChat Log: Tom Kail

I managed to find Tom Kail, the main chap behind Vectagon, in the depths of an Autumn night and bugged him quite a bit. The conversation ranged from the Unity3D engine, OUYA, his plans for Vectagon&IGF to his future plans as a university student, unfinished projects and touchscreen phones. As it has somehow become a Rawket Lawncher tradition, we finished with some choose between the two questions. Unedited, uncut, raw material inside ladies and gentlemen!
