This is currently a work in progress. Nothing is finished yet, not even the "completed" albums. If you want to download an album, keep in mind that it won't stay up-to-date for long. The songs probably won't change (aside from slight volume tweaks), but I'm constantly rewriting the descriptions, and the titles may not stay the same either.
